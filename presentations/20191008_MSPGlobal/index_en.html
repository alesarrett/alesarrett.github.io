Science and research for MSP

Why is it important?

  • intedisciplinarity of the marine environment
  • understanding/management of uncertainty
  • complexity of marine uses
  • scientific and technological improvements
  • expert judgment to support decision knowledge/making

How to realize an updated and efficient MSP data management?

  • identification of priority data to be manged for MSP
  • official/validated data from responsible authorities
  • open data sharing through standard and interoperable tools and services
  • explicit workflow for a shared, integrated and cross-border update of information
  • web portals for aggregation and tools for analysis
